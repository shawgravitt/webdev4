A favorite among locals and visitors, Picolos - The Red Bar is a must-do when in Grayton Beach!
The Red Bar is the area's most popular indoor destination. Its the place for fun with friends and family while taking in all of the eclectic decor, listening to the great live Jazz Music and enjoying great food and cocktails.
The Red Bar Jazz Band plays almost nightly. The party gets cranked up a notch after the dinner crowd thins creating a more funky atmosphere. Dont be surprised to spot celebrities here late at night. But please, everybody just wants to chill at The Red Bar.
Located in Grayton Beach, Florida -- The Red Bar is just a short drive from Destin & Panama City Beach. Housed in the old General Store at 70 Hotz Avenue - Downtown Grayton Beach.
